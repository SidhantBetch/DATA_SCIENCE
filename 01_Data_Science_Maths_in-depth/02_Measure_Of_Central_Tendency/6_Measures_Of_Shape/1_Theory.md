# 🧚‍♂️ Skewness
- Skewness measures the asymmetry of the distribution.
- A skewness value of 0 indicates a perfectly symmetrical distribution.
- Positive skewness indicates that the distribution is skewedto the right(i.e the tail is longer on the right), while negative skewness indicates a left skewness (i.e the tail is longer on the left)

$$
Skewness = \frac {\sum (x_i - x)^3}{(N-1).\sigma^3}
$$

## 🥈 Frequency & Cumulative Distribution
- A frequency distribution is a table that shows the number of occurrences of different values in a dataset.
- A cumulative distribution shows the accumulation of frequencles as you move though the values

example:
 value Range  |  Frequency  | Cumulative Distribution 
 -------------|-------------|-------------------------
 10-20        |  5          |  5
 20-30        |  8          |  5+8 = 13
 30-40        |  12         |  13+12 = 25
 40-50        |  6          |  25+6 = 31
