# 🥇What is STATISTICS ?
Statistics is a set of mathematical methods and tools that enagle us to answer important questions about data. 

## It is devided into two categories:

### 🥇 Descriptive statistics:- 
This offers methods to summarised data by transforming raw observations into meaningful information that is easy to interpret and share.

#### What we lean in Desceiptive ?
- Measures of Central Tendency
- Measures of Variability
- Measures of shape
- Percentiles
- Frequency Distribution
- Covariance and Correlation

  
### 🥈 Inferential statistics:- 
This offers methods to study experiments done on small samples of chalk out the inferences to the entire propulation(Entire domain).

#### What we lean in Interential ?
- Central Limit Theorem
- Hypothesis Testing
    - Z-Test
    - T-test
    - Chi square Test
