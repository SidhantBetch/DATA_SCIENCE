# ⚛️ Normal Distribution
It known as the Gaussian Distribution, is a probability distribution that is symmetric about the mean, showing that data near the mean are more frequent in occurrence than data for from the mean. 
In graph form, normal distribution will appear as a bell curve.

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2\pi}} \, e^{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x - \mu}{\sigma}\right)^2}
$$

where,  
$\mu = Mean or expectation of the distribution (and also its median and mode)$  
$\sigma = Standard deviation$  
$\sigma^2 = Variance of the distribution$
